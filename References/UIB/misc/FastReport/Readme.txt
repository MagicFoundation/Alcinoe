I) Prerequisites : 
   ---------------

Make sure you have FastReport 3.19+ and UIB2 (SVN) installed.


II) Installation instructions :
    ---------------------------

1) Copy to FastReport 3 "source\UIB" directory
2) Open frxUIB7.dpr (Delphi 7)
     or frxUIB9.bdsproj (Delphi 2005)
     or frxUIB10.bdsgroup (Delphi 2006)

   Build. Output files are redirected to ..\..\LibD[Version]

3) Open dclfrxUIB7.dpr (Delphi 7)
     or dclfrxUIB9.bdsproj (Delphi 2005)
     or dclfrxUIB10.bdsproj (Delphi 2006)

  Build. Install.

You now have a new frxUIBComponents component in your components palette. Drop it on the form with frxReport and frxDesigner to make UIB Components for FastReport available in the FastReports designer at runtime.
